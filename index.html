positions

There are 5 lanes: Top, Middle, Bottom, and Jungle.

How to win

There are 3 turrets in one lane. After breaking all the turrets, there is a new building called inhibitor.
If the inhibitor breaks, the Super Minion comes out. Then, there are the Twin Turrets, and when you break the Nexus, you win!
